ABOUT US

We are delivering the fresh meat since 1995 without being established online services, since we started serving you authentic Mansoor Country Chicken not even once have we increased our prices in spite of input costs going up for our farmers and for our operations. To continue to be operational sustainably, and to serve you the most authentic country chickens we are compelled to pass on a proportionate increase in price.For example the whole chicken at 950 grams was priced at Rs 987 and will be now Rs 1091. This slight change in price will be effective 1st March and we hope you support in our endeavour to help farmers and provide clean, ethical and environmentally sustainable meat products to your doorstep.
